About me!

Greetings, fair travelers and noble adventurers! I am Sir Percival, a valiant and fearless knight from the noble lineage of Valoria. My heart is filled with the burning fire of valor, and I stand unwaveringly by the code of chivalry. Throughout my illustrious journey to become a renowned hero, I have faced countless adventures and adversaries, all of which I have conquered with unparalleled grace and skill, or so I like to believe. Ah, the tales of my daring escapades are known far and wide. I have rescued damsels in distress (or occasionally, what I thought were damsels but turned out to be woodland creatures), charged fearlessly into battle, and displayed combat prowess that left foes in awe. There was this one time when I vanquished a horde of marauding goblins who dared to threaten a local farm, though I may have accidentally knocked down the farmer's barn during the process. And then there was the sacred relic rescue, which some might claim involved a cheese wheel mix-up, but I assure you, that was purely a jest!
